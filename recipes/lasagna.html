Lasagna



Homemade Lasagna.

This lasagna is simple, easy to make and delicious!

Ingredients:

  • .5 pound ground pork, .5 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs - 2 tbsp parsley, 1 clove garlic
  • Dash of sugar
  • Spices - dried basil, dried oregano, salt and black pepper
  • Noodles
  • Mozzarella, Parmesan and cottage cheese.
  • Eggs
